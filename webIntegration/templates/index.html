Heart Disease Analysis

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide and its cases are increasing due to unhealthy lifestyles, poor diet, stress, and lack of physical activity. Early identification of risk factors can significantly help in preventing severe heart conditions. This Heart Disease Analysis System uses data analytics and visualization techniques to study important health indicators such as age, blood pressure, cholesterol level, and heart rate. By analyzing these medical factors, the system provides insights that help users understand their potential risk level. The project integrates interactive data visualization using Tableau dashboards to explore heart disease trends and patterns. These visual insights help healthcare professionals, researchers, and individuals better understand risk factors and take preventive actions for maintaining heart health.

About Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to a group of conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels, including coronary artery disease, heart attack, and heart failure. Many of these conditions develop gradually and are strongly influenced by lifestyle factors such as smoking, poor diet, high cholesterol, obesity, and lack of exercise. The purpose of this project is to analyze heart disease data and present meaningful insights using data visualization tools. By transforming raw healthcare data into interactive dashboards, it becomes easier to understand patterns, identify high-risk groups, and highlight the major factors contributing to heart disease. Using Tableau dashboards, the system visually represents medical indicators such as age distribution, cholesterol levels, blood pressure patterns, and other risk factors. These insights help support better health awareness, preventive strategies, and data-driven decision making for individuals and healthcare professionals.
